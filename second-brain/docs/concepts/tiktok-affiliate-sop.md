## üéØ Daily Workflow

### Step 1: Morning Research (30 mins)
**Time:** Start of shift  
**Tool:** Kalodata.com

#### Actions:
1. **Log in to Kalodata**
   - URL: https://kalodata.com
   - Check trending products in Indonesia
   - Filter by: Last 7 days, High commission rate (>10%)

2. **Identify 4-6 Potential Products**
   - Look for products with:
     - >1000 sales in past week
     - Commission rate ‚â• 10%
     - Visual appeal (can demo in video)
     - Price point: 50K-500K IDR (sweet spot)

3. **Check Competitor Videos**
   - Search product name on TikTok
   - Analyze top 3 performing videos
   - Note: Hook style, duration, CTA used

4. **Record Research**
   - Update [[Content Calendar]] with selected products
   - Screenshot top competitor videos for reference

### Step 2: Script Writing (45 mins)
**For each of 4 videos:**

#### Template Structure (30-45 seconds):
```
[0-3s] HOOK (stop the scroll)
[3-10s] PROBLEM (pain point)
[10-25s] SOLUTION (product demo)
[25-35s] PROOF (results/testimony)
[35-45s] CTA (click link di bio)
```

#### Hook Options (pick one):
- "Jujur, gw kaget pas nyobain ini..."
- "Barang 50 ribu yang ngebantu hidup gw"
- "STOP beli yang mahal-mahal, ini lebih worth it"
- "Rahasia gw tetap [benefit] tanpa mahal"
- "POV: Lu nemuin barang yang [benefit]"

#### CTA Templates:
- "Link di bio kalau mau samaan"
- "Cek pinned comment buat link"
- "Klik link di bio sebelum sold out"
- "Drop ‚ù§Ô∏è kalau mau part 2"

#### Recording:
- Save scripts in [[Content Calendar]] under today's date
- Use consistent naming: `YYYY-MM-DD-ProductName`

---

### Step 3: Video Production (2-3 hours)
**Target:** 4 videos  
**Time per video:** ~30-45 mins

#### Setup Checklist:
- [ ] Lighting: Ring light or natural window light
- [ ] Background: Clean, minimal distraction
- [ ] Phone: Fully charged + storage space
- [ ] Product: Clean, ready to demonstrate
- [ ] Script: Printed or visible on second screen

#### Filming Guidelines:
1. **Vertical format** (9:16)
2. **1080p minimum** resolution
3. **Face to camera** for first 3 seconds (builds trust)
4. **Show product in action** (not just holding)
5. **B-roll footage** for editing flexibility

#### Quick Filming Tips:
- Film 2-3 takes per segment (gives editing options)
- Speak slightly faster than normal (energy)
- Show your face = higher trust = better conversion
- Demonstrate the actual use case

---

### Step 4: Editing (1-2 hours)
**Apps:** CapCut (recommended) or TikTok native

#### Editing Checklist:
- [ ] Hook appears in first 3 seconds
- [ ] Add captions/subtitles (90% watch without sound)
- [ ] Use trending sounds (check TikTok trending)
- [ ] Add text overlays for key points
- [ ] Zoom in on product details
- [ ] Speed up boring parts (unboxing, etc.)
- [ ] End card with CTA visible for 3+ seconds

#### Caption Guidelines:
- Font: Bold, readable (Arial Bold or system default)
- Color: White with black stroke (always readable)
- Position: Lower third (don't cover your face)
- Timing: Sync with speech

#### Music Selection:
- Check TikTok Creative Center for trending sounds
- Use sounds that match the energy of the product
- Save favorite sounds to a playlist for quick access

---

### Step 5: Upload & Optimization (30 mins)
**Platform:** TikTok

#### Upload Checklist:
- [ ] Video file named with product (for your reference)
- [ ] Caption includes keywords
- [ ] Hashtags: 3-5 relevant tags
- [ ] Cover image shows product clearly
- [ ] Pinned comment with CTA
- [ ] Link in bio updated (coordinate with Amos)

#### Caption Template:
```
[Hook statement as first line]

[2-3 line story/context]

#affiliate #productreview #[category] #[brand] #[producttype]
```

#### Best Posting Times (Indonesia):
- **12:00-13:00** (lunch break)
- **17:00-19:00** (after work/school)
- **20:00-22:00** (prime time)

Space out uploads: 2 morning, 2 evening

---

### Step 6: Engagement (Ongoing)
**Time:** Throughout the day (in between filming)

#### Actions:
- Reply to comments within 1 hour (boosts algorithm)
- Pin comment with link reminder
- Like replies to encourage more engagement
- Check DMs for product questions

## üìä Weekly Review Process

**Every Sunday (or Monday morning):**

1. **Export Analytics**
   - Views per video
   - Engagement rate (likes + comments / views)
   - Click-through rate (if available)
   - Conversion/sales data from TikTok Shop

2. **Identify Winners**
   - Top 2 videos by views
   - Top 2 videos by conversion
   - What made them work? (document in [[Content Calendar]])

3. **Identify Losers**
   - Bottom 2 videos
   - What went wrong? (boring hook? bad lighting?)
   - Don't repeat those mistakes

4. **Plan Next Week**
   - Double down on what worked
   - Test 1 new hook style
   - Try 1 new product category
