# TikTok Affiliate Business

## Overview

Amos's content business generating passive income through TikTok affiliate marketing.

## Current Performance

- **Monthly Revenue:** 15-25 juta IDR
- **Platform:** TikTok Shop Affiliate
- **Duration:** Running for 1 year

## Team

| Role | Person | Details |
|------|--------|---------|
| Strategy/Owner | Amos | Direction, research, optimization |
| Content Creator | Adek | 4 videos/day, 3.5jt/month salary |

## Tools & Resources

- **Kalodata.com** — Trending product research
- **TikTok Shop** — Affiliate platform
- **Content pipeline** — 4 videos/day production

## Process

1. **Research** (Amos)
   - Use Kalodata to find trending products
   - Analyze competitor content
   - Identify winning angles

2. **Create** (Adek)
   - Film 4 videos/day
   - Edit and post
   - Monitor performance

3. **Optimize**
   - Review analytics
   - Double down on winners
   - Cut losers

## Goals

- **Maintain** current 15-25jt/month while building CuanBoss
- **Scale** if opportunity arises
- **Systematize** so it runs with minimal input

- Platform risk (TikTok algorithm changes)
- Content burnout

- Competition increasing
