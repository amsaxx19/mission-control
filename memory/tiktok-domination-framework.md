## AUTHENTICITY CHECKLIST

Biar gak cringe, ikutin ini:

- [ ] Show real struggle, bukan cuma pamer
- [ ] Admit mistakes & failures
- [ ] Gak over-polished (jalan2 di rumah ✅)
- [ ] Raw emotion: marah, sedih, bingung, excited
- [ ] Gak jual penderitaan, tapi jual perjuangan
- [ ] Respond to hate comments dengan data/logic
- [ ] Show wife/family reactions (real conflict)

---

## TIMOTHY RONALD PRINCIPLE

**"Plan every word. Loved or hated, never middle."**

Setiap video sebelum record:
1. **Hook** — First 3 detik harus stop scroll
2. **Story arc** — Beginning, conflict, resolution
3. **Emotional trigger** — Anger, curiosity, relatability
4. **Takeaway** — Apa yang audience bawa pulang?
5. **CTA** — Like, comment, follow, check link
