# Example: Marketing Campaign Workflow

This example demonstrates using the workflow system for marketing and copywriting tasks.

## Scenario

**Business Need:** Launch a new affiliate marketing course. Need landing page, email sequence, ad copy, and social media content.

**Timeline:** 1 week

**Deliverables:**

- 5-email onboarding sequence

## Tips for Marketing Workflows

1. **Start with research** - Understand audience and competitors
2. **Define voice early** - Consistency across all channels
3. **Test variations** - A/B test headlines and CTAs
4. **Track everything** - Know what works
5. **Iterate fast** - Update based on performance data
