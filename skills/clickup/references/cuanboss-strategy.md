# CuanBoss — Platform Strategy

## Vision
"Whop.com for Indonesia" — Creator marketplace platform

## Business Model
- **Revenue:** Transaction fee per sale (10-20%)
- **Marketplace:**
  - Creators sell digital products (courses, ebooks, templates)
  - Brands list clipping/content creation jobs
  - Clippers claim jobs & get paid

## Tech Stack
- Frontend: Vercel
- Database: Supabase
- IDE: Antigravity (vibecoding)
- Domain: cuanboss.id

## Current Status (Jan 2026)
- 80% development complete
- Need: Payment gateway integration
- Pre-launch phase

## Competitor Analysis

### Direct Competitors (Indo)
- **Tokopedia/Shopee:** General marketplace, bukan creator-focused
- **Rupa Rupa:** Digital products tapi general
- **Siasat:** Course platform tapi closed ecosystem

### International
- **Whop.com:** The benchmark — clean, creator-focused
- **Gumroad:** Simple digital products
- **Patreon:** Subscription model

**Gap:** No dedicated creator marketplace in Indo with job marketplace + digital products combined.

## Go-to-Market Strategy

### Phase 1: Seed Sellers (Month 1-2)
Target 50 creators to list products
- TikTok creators with courses
- Designers with templates
- Coaches with ebooks

### Phase 2: Clipping Marketplace (Month 2-3)
Launch job board for clippers
- Brands post clipping needs
- Clippers apply & get paid

### Phase 3: Scale (Month 4+)
- Referral program
- Affiliate system
- Premium features

## Pricing Strategy

### For Sellers:
- Free: List up to 3 products, 15% fee
- Pro: 99k/month, unlimited products, 10% fee
- Business: 299k/month, white-label, 5% fee

### For Clipping Jobs:
- Free to post
- 10% fee when job completed

## Key Metrics to Track
- GMV (Gross Merchandise Value)

- Number of buyers
- Transaction volume
- CAC (Customer Acquisition Cost)
- LTV (Lifetime Value)
